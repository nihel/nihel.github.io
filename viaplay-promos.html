Billboard Promos

We feature a billboard on our start page across all platforms to showcase our content. To improve this, we include promos and a 15-second trailer, helping users discover something to watch and making our product feel more exciting and inspiring.

TV app recording

In the TV app, the player operates as a consistently positioned layer beneath the interface, we achieve smooth transitions between the start and product pages even while the promo is playing.

Website recording

The promos on the billboard were displayed for only a week. To maximize the efforts invested, we linked the promos to the product ID. This allowed us to play the promos on the product pages, ensuring continued visibility and impact.

iOS recording
